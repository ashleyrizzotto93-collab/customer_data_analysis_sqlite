**SQL Customer Data Analysis**
----------------------------------------

**Tools+Database**
Sqlite and a Kaggle public data set including sales data covering customer activities in 2015,
I conducted introductory functions to analyze mutiple properties of this data set including (but not limited to):


1) Total revenue made on orders
2) Top customers of all time
3) Most active ordering customers in the past week 
4) Average of customers
5) Sum of customer orders

Kaggle Data Set: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/a4893162ebfcc446989d1e83b85d34d9104b9ab5a105d444b71afae8282b2a22
